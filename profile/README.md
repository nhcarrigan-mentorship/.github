# NHCarrigan Mentorship

This repository houses the resume-worthy showcase projects that members of our mentorship programme are working on.

## What is this programme?

Our mentorship programme gives participants:
- Special access to a dedicated channel in our Discord, where you can ask us questions and connect with your fellow mentees.
- A dedicated repository in our GitHub mentorship organisation where we will work with you on a project to showcase on your resume.
- Weekly accountability calls to help you stay on track with your goals.
- Mentee-specific events and workshops to help you elevate your skills, prepare for interviews, and more!
